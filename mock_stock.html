Mock Stock

  1. Each participant/team will be allotted a virtual budget to bid for items.
  2. Once a bid is placed, it cannot be withdrawn or altered.
  3. Participants must be present throughout the session.
  4. Items purchased must be strategically chosen for profit maximization.
  5. Final evaluation will be based on your total portfolio value and reasoning.
  6. Any unfair or disruptive behavior will lead to disqualification.
  7. The judge’s decision will be final and binding.
