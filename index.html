The Department of Economics

The Honours course in Economics was introduced in the college in 1932. Students studying Economics (Honours) get a plethora of opportunities starting from being in academics, banking sector, government services, though the majority of students are now getting employed in Data Analytics which is a booming service industry with ample scope of growth. Asutosh College, because of its convenient access to many forms of transportation and strong academic standing of the department, has always attracted the students not only of its neighborhood but also of the entire state. Most of the students who have completed their graduation from this institution are now placed in prestigious organizations throughout the globe. Along with the traditional method of classroom teaching the department takes utmost care in meeting students' academic queries.The department also organizes students' seminars, workshops and invited lecture sessions regularly. The members of faculty participate in international, national and state-level seminars, conferences and workshops on a regular basis as part of Faculty Development Programmes. Students of different semesters too participate in various events and competitions organized by other academic institutions outside the college. Apart from the regular class rooms in the main building of the college, the department has a dedicated classroom with a projector in the humanities block. The department also maintains a specialised seminar library for the students of the Economics (Honours). At present this library has 564 books covering more or less all the papers taught at the undergraduate level. In addition to this, students of economics (Honours) can also access both the Central Library and the reading room library of the college which has 634 and 84 books respectively on the subject. New publications are also added to the library at regular intervals so that the students can have the knowledge of the latest developments in the subject. Students who have graduated from the department in the last academic session have displayed a spectacular result with none of them scoring less than 60% and the highest being more than 90%. The performance of the students of the ongoing semesters in the university examinations is also noteworthy.The department also has a glorious past with many notable alumni. For instance, Sri Debashis Biswas, Inspector of colleges of the University of Calcutta to which this college is affiliated, has graduated from this college. In a nutshell, the department provides an encouraging academic and intellectual ambience for prospective students.
